## 🚀 Getting Started

### Loading Screen
Every time you open the app, a cinematic boot sequence plays:
- Terminal-style system initialization log
- Progress bar filling to 100%
- "ARISE" reveal animation
- Auto-dismisses into the main app in ~3.5 seconds

### Daily Quest Popup
Immediately after loading, a **Quest Info popup** appears showing:
- Your Morning / Evening / Daily task checklists
- You can check off tasks directly from the popup
- Daily quest completion counter
- A rotating motivational "CAUTION" message
- Dismiss with "⚡ ARISE — BEGIN YOUR QUEST"

## 👤 Profile Panel (HUNTER tab)

### Identity
- **Name** — editable
- **Avatar** — icon with rank-colored glow
- **Title** — unlocks as you level up (16 titles from "The Awakened" to "National Level Hunter")
- **Level badge** + **Rank badge** — both color-coded to current rank

### XP & Leveling
- **XP Bar** — shows current XP / XP needed for next level
- XP required per level **increases with each rank** (harder to level up as you progress)
- **Rank Progress Bar** — shows how far through your current rank you are, and levels until next rank

### Rank Ladder (8 ranks)
| Rank | Levels | Color | XP/Level |
|------|--------|-------|----------|
| E-Rank | 1–24 | Gray | 100 |
| D-Rank | 25–49 | Blue | 150 |
| C-Rank | 50–74 | Green | 200 |
| B-Rank | 75–99 | Purple | 300 |
| A-Rank | 100–124 | Orange | 500 |
| S-Rank | 125–149 | Gold | 800 |
| SS-Rank | 150–199 | Red | 1200 |
| National Level | 200+ | White | 2000 |

### Rank-Up Ceremony
When you cross a rank threshold, a **full-screen cinematic overlay** appears:
- Burst ring animation
- Rank icon, label, and title
- Description of your new rank
- Tap to dismiss

### Body Stats
- Height, Weight, BMI (auto-calculated)
- Total XP Earned (lifetime)

### Weight Log
- Log your weight with the `+` button
- Mini line chart showing last 14 entries
- Delta indicator (green = lost, red = gained)

### Level History
- Collapsible log of every level-up with date and rank

### Data Backup
- **EXPORT** — downloads your entire save as a `.json` file
- **IMPORT** — restores from a backup file
- Use this to protect your data or move between devices

### Reset Day
- Resets all morning/evening/daily tasks to uncompleted for a fresh day

## 💾 Data & Storage

- All data saved to **localStorage** (persists across sessions)
- Schema version system — auto-migrates on updates
- **Export** your data as `.json` anytime from Profile panel
- **Import** to restore from backup
- Works offline after first load
- Can be installed as a PWA (Add to Home Screen on mobile)
